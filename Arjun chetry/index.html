About

Arjun Chetry is the current president of All Assam Gorkha Student Union (AAGSU). A group that focuses on the platform of student welfare, social welfare, and cultural growth. Arjun Chetry joined as a president on 8th October 2020. Before this, he had also served as the vice president of AAGSU for 6 years from 2014-2020. ... Arjun has completed his graduation in MBA from Sikkim Manipal University. Being a social activist he has worked on various social issues by actively taking part in it and helping people out.since his twenties, he has been a part of AAGSU and has done a lot of hard work towards its success.
